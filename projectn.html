Our Story

As they say marriages are planned in heaven,that is so evident in my life. All I had to do was to focus on the walk with Christ. We both have been born and brought up in the same place for so many years but never came across each other. That is how God kept us hidden from each other moulded us individually made us whole and brought us together. Before I could meet Niklesh God revealed me the month we were going to encounter each other.i.e November through a dream. But initially when I saw him I wasn't convicted that this is from God. Later when that day arrived from no where for a conversation. I was deeply convicted and full of peace about the Father's plan towards my marriage. And from that day every moment every step towards marriage was a miracle, something magical and wonderful. Things slided away so easily and quickly and everything that wasn't possible in my eyes became possible in front of my very own eyes.... Experiencing God's grace and favour in abundance over my marriage with this amazingly dynamic person❣️with a humble and grateful heart.
